About HAUQALA 2025

HAUQALA ‘25 is the official arts fest of Darunnajath Islamic Complex, proudly presented by the ITHIHAD FRIENDS. Rooted in the spirit of ITHIHAD — meaning unity — this event reflects the harmony and brotherhood taught by Islam.


The event stands as a reminder that every skill, every voice, and every idea is a gift from Allah ﷻ — and when used right, it becomes a way to spread goodness. Through performances, exhibitions, and competitions, students express their talents while staying rooted in faith and character.


Within the campus, the festival creates an atmosphere of excitement and inspiration. The halls fill with energy, laughter, and the joy of participation — yet beneath it all lies a deeper intention: to honor Allah through positive expression and good deeds.
